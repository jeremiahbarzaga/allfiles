Lingap Bata Center is an all-boys orphanage located in San Jose, Patag, Santa Maria, Bulacan. It is the first Sta, Maria, Bulacan Welfare Foundation that was founded by Mr. Reynaldo SL. Perez, a president in Fiesta Committee, established on 16th of July year 2000.

The source of income for sustaining the basic needs of the shelter comes from the collection of Marian Novenas from Parish La Purisima Conception and also from visitors and benefactors.

The centers mission was to provide temporary substitute care to the male children who cannot temporarily or permanently stay in their homes, in an institution that can provide them with opportunities for their growth and development.

Vision

To provide temporarily family care,love,nurturance,development and protection to children who cannot remain in their homes.
To maintain a residential center where the basic needs, as well as the emotional,social,intellectual and spiritual needs of the children are met.
To design and implement an individual, unique case management.Plan for each children
To create home where children can benefit an established framework of general routines and where individual boundaries of bahavior are well defined from the children,to volunteer,staff and others professions.
To provide formal education as well as non formal education training.
To develop permanency plans for each child either return to his home or a more appropriate alternative placement as soon as possible when the child is no longer at risk or in need of the center help.

About us

Lingap Bata Center is located in Sta. Maria, Bulacan. It is an all boys orphanage maintained and supported by the Immaculate Parish and some volunteers. The children are aged 4 and older. They are sent to school and are taught by volunteers of extra curricular activities.
